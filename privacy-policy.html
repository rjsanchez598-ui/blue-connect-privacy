BlueConnect Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2025

BlueConnect (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, BlueConnect, and any related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from users:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Photos: If you upload a profile picture or other images within the app.
  • Location Information: To help connect you with local opportunities and users.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as pages viewed, actions taken, and device details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your BlueConnect account
  • Personalize your experience and improve our services
  • Connect you with local jobs or users
  • Communicate with you about updates or support
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of the app

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (such as hosting or analytics)
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with law or protect user safety

We never share your photos, personal details, or location with advertisers.

4. Data Retention and Security

We keep your information only as long as necessary for app functionality or legal requirements. We use industry-standard encryption and secure storage methods to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access or update your account information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw location permissions at any time in your device settings

To make a privacy request, contact us at blueconnect@gmail.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

BlueConnect is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
